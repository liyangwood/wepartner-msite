About Us

- A ChinaGate Company

ChinaGate was founded in 1998 in Silicon Valley, the U.S. hub for innovative startups. We are the market leader in connecting global brands with Chinese consumers worldwide. We reach Chinese all over the world, and own the largest Chinese portal site outside China. We have proven success in helping brands expand their presence and achieve their marketing and sales goals in the ever-growing Chinese market.

Fully holding the largest overseas Chinese Community Portal Site wenxuecity.com, ChinaGate reaches Chinese audiences all over the globe, covering over 1/3 of the total Chinese population in North America on a daily basis.

International operation: with tech support and promotion teams in China and core business entity in the U.S, ChinaGate connects the two biggest economies in the world using the internet, mobile technology and social media.

We offer turn-key integrated marketing communication plans and branding consulting services, to customize the WeChat package for each individual client. Hence our client can get the most of the opportunity in line with their business goals.
